Delete Your TaskWise Account

You can delete your TaskWise account and all associated data directly from within the app, or by contacting us.

Option 1 — Delete from the app (Recommended)

1. Open TaskWise
2. Tap the menu icon (☰) in the top left
3. Go to Settings
4. Scroll down to Data Management
5. Tap Delete Account
6. Confirm deletion in the dialog

Option 2 — Contact us

Email us at your@email.com with subject line "Delete My Account" and we will delete your account immediately.

What gets deleted

  • ✅ Your Firebase authentication account
  • ✅ All reminders and tasks you created
  • ✅ All categories you created
  • ✅ All local app data on your device

What is retained

  • No personal data is retained after account deletion
